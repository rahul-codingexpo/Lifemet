FAQ

What is LifeMet Group?
LifeMet Group is a full-service agency specializing in third-party manufacturing for pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and ayurvedic products. We simplify the entire process — from formulation and product design to packaging, branding, and delivery — acting as your single point of contact and ensuring your product is market-ready with minimal effort.
Who We Work With?
Brands & Founders:
Looking to manufacture, launch, or scale pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and ayurvedic products domestically or globally.
Manufacturing Facilities:
Seeking foreign clients and long-term partnerships to expand their production footprint.
How does our process work?
We make healthcare product creation simple, seamless, and risk-free.

Product Design & Development:
We help you bring your product to life, whether you choose from our curated portfolio of market-tested products or want a fully custom formulation. Our brand specialists guide every step of design, from the formulation and functional benefits to packaging concepts, ensuring your product aligns perfectly with your brand identity and market requirements.
Sampling & Approval
Before production begins, we provide product samples for your review. You approve the formulation, packaging, and branding, giving you complete confidence that the final product meets your expectations. Our process ensures minimal effort on your side while maintaining maximum clarity and quality control.
Production & Delivery
Once approved, our agency manages the entire production process, coordinating with verified manufacturing partners and overseeing quality, compliance, and timelines. We deliver the finished products directly to your warehouse, ready for launch — domestic or international — so you can focus on scaling your brand rather than managing vendors.
What are the advantages of choosing Lifemet Group?
LifeMet Group redefines third-party manufacturing by providing a truly integrated, end-to-end service under one roof that few agencies offer. We combine formulation expertise, product development, packaging, branding, and compliance management into a seamless workflow, so brands never have to manage multiple vendors or risk delays. With hands-on quality oversight, fast sample approvals, and reliable delivery, we make launching or scaling pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and ayurvedic products efficient, confident, and stress-free — all while maintaining the highest industry standards.
